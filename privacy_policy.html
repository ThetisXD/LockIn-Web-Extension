Privacy Policy for LockIn Web Extension

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

LockIn Web Extension is designed to help users stay focused through timer tools and optional website blocking features. Your privacy is important, and this extension is built to operate entirely on your device.

Information that the developer Do Not Collect

LockIn does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data. All settings, timers, and blacklist entries remain on your device using the browser’s built‑in storage.

Permissions Used

The extension uses the following browser permissions:

  • storage – Saves timer settings and your tab blacklist locally.
  • tabs – Reads the URLs of open browser tabs solely to check them against your blacklist. This information is processed locally and never stored or transmitted.
  • sidePanel – Required to display the extension’s interface in the browser side panel.

These permissions are used strictly for the extension’s functionality and never for data collection or tracking.

Data Sharing

No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or the developer. All functionality occurs locally within your browser.
